Specificity can be reduced when people leave negative reviews that do not necessarily reflect an app's functionality (e.g., "this app sux because it does not have a purple icon and purple iz the best colur"). Occasionally, though, an app will have a clear trail of valid, negative reviews. Any such app was excluded.
